Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: Username, email, display name, profile photo.

Usage Data: Messages sent, posts created, features used, login times.

Device Information: Browser type, device type (for analytics only).

Content: Messages, posts, comments, reactions, voice notes, images you share.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Service
  • To send notifications about messages and activity
  • To personalize your experience
  • To prevent abuse, spam, and violations
  • To provide analytics to administrators (aggregate, non-personal)

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud). Messages are stored encrypted in transit. Secret Chat messages are end-to-end encrypted and burn after reading.

4. Data Retention

  • Chat messages: Auto-deleted after 1 month (last 10 per conversation kept)
  • Account data: Deleted permanently after 30-day grace period
  • Activity logs: Retained for 90 days for analytics
  • Deleted accounts: All data permanently erased

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or rent your personal data. We may share data:

  • With legal authorities if required by law
  • To protect rights, property, or safety of MR Group, users, or others
  • In aggregate, non-personal form for analytics

6. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all data (30-day grace period)
  • Disable notifications
  • Use the Service without sharing location

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including Firestore Security Rules, Cloud Functions validation, rate limiting, 2FA support, and end-to-end encryption for Secret Chat.

8. Cookies

MR Chat uses local storage (not cookies) to remember your theme preference, sound settings, and login session. No tracking cookies are used.

9. Children's Privacy

MR Chat is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact: MR Group
